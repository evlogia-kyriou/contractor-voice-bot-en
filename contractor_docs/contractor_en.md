# ABC Plumbing & HVAC Services

## Services

- Plumbing installation and repair
- Pipe replacement and leak detection
- Water heater installation and repair
- HVAC installation, maintenance, and repair
- Air conditioning tune-up and servicing
- Drain cleaning and unclogging
- Emergency plumbing services

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do you provide free estimates?**
Yes, we provide free estimates for all installations and projects over $500.
For service calls and repairs, a $75 diagnostic fee applies.

**How long does a typical appointment take?**
Most plumbing repairs take 1 to 2 hours. HVAC maintenance visits take
approximately 1 hour. Major installations may take a full day.

**Do you warranty your work?**
Yes. All labor is warranted for 12 months. Parts carry the manufacturer
warranty which is typically 1 to 5 years depending on the product.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Payment is due upon
completion of work. Financing is available for projects over $2,000.

**Are you licensed and insured?**
Yes. We are fully licensed in Texas and carry $2 million in general
liability insurance and workers compensation coverage.

**Can I book an appointment outside business hours?**
Emergency services are available 24/7 at the emergency rate. Standard
appointments can only be booked during business hours.
